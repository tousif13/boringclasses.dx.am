ECET


ECET is known as Engineering CommonEntrance Test which will be conducted by JNTUA on behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education for Diploma holders of Engineering/Pharmacy/B.Sc for admissions through lateral entry at 2nd year level into B.E/B.Tech/B.pharmacy in respective academic years.
